About Us

Mission

1. To prepare students to excel in their chosen professions by offering high-quality education in automobile engineering with fundamental knowledge, interdisciplinary problem-solving skills and confidence required.
2. To provide a supportive and diverse environment that encourages students to achieve the best of their abilities to be innovators or job providers.
3. To maintain constant and active partnerships with industries for technology development and transfer through consultancy projects.

Vision

“To be a premier department in Automobile engineering and reach the highest academic level in the field of Automobile Engineering by imparting knowledge, continuously enhancing Research & Development activities, supporting industries through consultancy programmes and providing the nation with high-quality engineers”
